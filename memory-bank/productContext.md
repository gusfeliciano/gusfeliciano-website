# Product Context

> Why this project exists from the user's perspective. The job to be done.
> Update when the user's needs or the UX direction shifts.
>
> Scope boundary: the *user, their job, and how it should feel* live here.
> *What we're building and why, plus scope* live in `projectbrief.md`.

## The user

1. Recruiters / hiring managers evaluating Gus for TPM / cloud roles.
2. Tech peers arriving from blog posts (cloud resume challenge, OWASP write-ups).
3. Developers forking the repo as a portfolio template.

## The job

Quickly answer "who is Gus and what has he done?" — then read his writing or reach out.

## How they solve it today

LinkedIn / resume PDF — flat, not differentiated, no writing samples.

## How this should feel

Clean, minimalist, content-first. Readable in both light and dark mode. Mobile-friendly.

## Anti-goals

- Not a heavyweight "personal brand" site with newsletters/popups
- Not a generic template demo — personal content stays front and center
